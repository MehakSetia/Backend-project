Taj Mahal Visitor Information

  • Location: Taj Mahal, Dharmapuri, Forest Colony, Tajganj, Agra, Uttar Pradesh 282001, India.
  • Weather: Ideal visiting time: October to March (cooler months).
  • Timings: Opens 30 minutes before sunrise, Closes 30 minutes after sunset, Closed on Fridays.
  • Entry Fee: Indian Citizens: INR 50, Foreign Visitors: INR 1100, Children Below 15 Years: Free entry.
  • Best Viewing Times: Early morning at sunrise, Late evening during sunset, Full moon nights offer a special viewing opportunity.

Victoria Memorial Visitor Information

  • Location: Victoria Memorial, 1, Queens Way, Maidan, Kolkata, West Bengal 700071, India.
  • Weather: Ideal visiting time: October to March (cooler months).
  • Timings: Museum Hours: 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM (Closed on Mondays and National Holidays), Garden Hours: 5:30 AM to 6:15 PM.
  • Entry Fee: Indian Citizens: INR 30 (Gardens), INR 20 (Museum), Foreign Visitors: INR 500 (Museum and Gardens combined).
  • Best Viewing Times: Early morning for a peaceful garden visit, Evening for a stroll through the illuminated gardens and view of the lit-up monument.
